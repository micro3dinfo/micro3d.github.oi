About us

MICRO3D

Excellence in Additive Manufacturing

Micro3D is a renowned organization engaged in manufacturing, supplying, trading & repair service of 3D printing services since 2020, located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The company was setup by two aspiring people with rich experience in Additive manufacturing. We have a young and energetic team having positive approach to offer fast and reliable after sales service and also provide engineer insulation support.

Micro3D is a additive manufacturing industry deals with 3D Printing Services over FDM Technology and we are manufacturing and customizing the FDM printer , SLA technology to provide many areas , education, health care R&D, dental & jwellery . Our company provides prototypes to industries over engineering, automotive, architecture, consumer goods, etc.

To enhances mass production through the 3d printing and to educate the new generations for the technology changes over existing learning and manufacturing processes

To Design and develop high quality product and services through emerging technology
